About Me

The artist behind the canvas

Hello, I'm Saumya

An artist who believes that art is the purest form of expression.

Art has always been my escape, my therapy, and my voice. From the first time I held a brush, I knew this was my path in life. Every piece I create tells a story - sometimes of joy, sometimes of sorrow, but always of truth.

My journey with art began in childhood, scribbling on any surface I could find. Today, I work across multiple mediums including digital art, traditional painting, sketches, and photography. Each medium offers its own unique way to express what words cannot.

Through my art, I aim to evoke emotions and inspire others to see the beauty in everyday moments. I believe everyone has an artist within them - sometimes we just need the right moment to let it out.

- Saumya

My Creative Journey

The Beginning

First Exhibition

Digital Expansion

Began exploring digital art and expanded my creative horizons to include digital illustrations and designs.

Continuing to Create

Always learning, growing, and pushing the boundaries of my creativity. Every day is a new opportunity to create something beautiful.
